---

This week Forza Horizon 6 came out, an extremely rare case of me buying a full-priced video game!!! I loved 4 and 5 so I was very excited going into this one, and not just because it's set in Japan this time! As expected, it's kinda more of the same as the last few, which I'm pretty okay with. I find all of the dialogue and writing to be incredibly cringe and the framing of the whole thing feels a little odd 6 entries in, like I always felt this way about the Assassin's Creed games, why not just set them in the time period in a shared universe without the modern day VR time travel framing device??? I don't know how much the Horizon series even needs their namesake festival anymore, like are we gaining anything by the game being structured around traveling to this country for a big music and car festival and being explictly a tourist? Just feels like a cop-out for writing a really lazy, surface-level version of each country. Like they keep doing this thing where the characters will tell you some one-line fact about the car you're driving or the area you're in that sounds interesting, but they never actually expand on it past "hey isn't it cool that this car is influential?" or "this temple is really old and important in japanese culture!" which like, sure, but I'm not sure how much I'm really gaining by them saying any of this.

But!!!!! Whatever!!! It's super fun so I can't complain too much. I've been intentionally keeping all of the cars I get in the class they start in, which means I have a ton of C/B/A-class cars and almost nothing above that, which I think is the more fun way to play anyway. I'll let it naturally progress up to S1 and above when the game wants me to 🙂‍↕️ I will most certainly be posting a screenshot collection when I've played more!
