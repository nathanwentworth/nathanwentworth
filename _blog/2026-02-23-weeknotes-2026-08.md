Wrote most of this over the weekend but finishing it up during this big blizzard, my windows are completely covered by snow!! I can't even see outside!! 

Got my replacement earbuds, the Nicehck EB2S (green, 3.5mm, with inline mic)! Very affordable on aliexpress, a little over 30 dollars with shipping, and wow they sound so nice. Very clear and crisp, surprisingly open and wide soundstage, and very comfortable! Only real issue I have with them is that there's a ton of sound leak, so I can't reasonably use them in the office where it can get pretty quiet, but they'll be perfect for at home or when walking around.
