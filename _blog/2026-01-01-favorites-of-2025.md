2025 was pretty good overall!!! I had a great trip to Japan to start the year and it was fun visiting mariah over the summer. I didn't achieve either goal I set out for myself, but whatever!! Next year I hope to lean a little more creative and less consumptive and spend less time scrolling twitter, but I'm also not going to be too hard on myself about it because whateverrrrrrrrr

## Hobbies and things I got into

- Got really into watching movies more this year, with 107 watched!! It's been fun really keeping up with new releases and also filling in the gaps of stuff I've wanted to watch for a long time but haven't
- Picked up guitar again after encouragement from one of my coworkers. Trying a different approach by following youtube tutorials instead of Rocksmith and honestly I feel much more confident with it already!
- Weeknotes has been really nice, it's basically entirely replaced Posting to me, even if it's not 100% the same thing, I still felt the desire to post but bluesky just wasn't doing it for me, for some reason. Also no better feeling than posting a weeknote post and having one of my friends message me about some part of it!! Much more satisfying than likes
- Ever since we got a ping pong table at work I've been really into it, many days after work I'll play for over an hour with one or a few of my coworkers. It's surprisingly good exercise!!! Especially since I'm running all over the office to get balls that fly past me lmao. I'm sure part of this interest comes from the Gear aspect of the sport, like there's so many types of rubbers and things to research, but it helps that it's very easily accessible and now that I've bought a paddle, free to play

Really excited to dig more into his films next year! I watched In the Mood for Love with Mariah when I was visiting her and I absolutely adored it!!!! I'm so curious about other Wong Kar Wai movies now and which of the two they more align to. Got to see Linda Linda Linda in theaters which I had never seen before, really loved it.

## Video Games

I barely played any games this year, but I did enjoy the ones I played!

- **Animal Well**
  - a perfect puzzle platformer type game, really fantastic puzzle and level design, great art style, highly recommend
- **To a T**
  - cute and fun!!!!! while i wasn't fully sold on where the plot ended up going, i really loved this. i hope keita takahashi can keep making games
- **Deltarune Chapters 3 & 4**
  - okay chapter 3 i admit i was not super into, but chapter 4 brought me back into it and i'm very curious where this plot is leading
- **Peak**
  - Peak is peak, a perfect friendslop game, i finally reached the peak in a run and it's immensely satisfying
- **Despelote**
  - really cool autobiographical game, shocked by how invested i got in the world cup results by the end lol
