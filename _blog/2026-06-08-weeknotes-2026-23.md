Okay yay I'm finally not sick anymore!!!!! On Monday I went to go see [Obsession](https://letterboxd.com/nathanwentworth/film/obsession-2025-2/) with a bunch of pinball people, great movie! Very spooky, pretty well done, after a point it does kinda feel like a "youtube university" project as Will Wilkinson says, where you can tell he's mostly directed short films and now needs to fill a 100 minute runtime, but the pacing and tension was great throughout so it's the most minor critique lol. Really exciting seening this new generation of directors being really successful. Hopefully all this early success (Obsession, Backrooms, etc) lets them get Weird with it or take their time and make things that are really interesting and different. I fully expect there to be a wave of films in the next few years that are very much written from the perspective of people who grew up on the internet and how that affects the way you interact with the world.

Sunday was another Halo 3 LAN party!!! I've really loved doing these!!!! Afterwards in my Scrolling time I saw [this tweet](https://x.com/PatrickMaka/status/2063714717831720965) of Xbox's "timeline of gamer rooms" and it's so incredibly tragic, the removal of the sofa in the last photo representing the full transition of video games from an in-person social experience to a solo/online experience, from the living room to the bedroom. So it's been really nice doing in-person video game days with people again!! Like sure it's nice that online games exist for my friends that I don't live near but I feel like these LAN parties are a great middleground where they're far enough apart where you can convince people to travel for them.
