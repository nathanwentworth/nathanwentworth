Big birding day on Sunday! We did a really similar route to [Saturday](/posts/weeknotes-2026-25/) but Sean came this time so we drove instead of train, and Olivia came along! We got a slightly late start but so did David, we all got to Pool Hill forest first, David had been there for 40 minutes already but was on the search for an indigo bunting that he had heard but not seen. He met us in the parking area and we walked in, still hearing that indigo bunting, and after about two minutes of walking we look up and there it is!! Really good moment

We left here and were off to get lunch on our way to Plum Island. Sadly we left a little too late and when we got to Little Wolf they weren't doing any coffee drinks since it was 15 minutes before closing, so I couldn't get the pour over I wanted. Sad!!!!! I'll just have to go again next time.

Plum Island however, really really good. We went pretty deep into the park to start to do a 1 mile loop. Moseying through we saw a bunch of birds, none super unique but some I haven't heard of before (Eastern Towhee, Lesser Yellowlegs). I was getting eaten up by bugs, mainly greenhead flies, so not like I have huge welts like from a mosquito but still annoying!! One of the biggest things for me though was I tested out shooting consistently at +1/3 and on a different film profile on my fuji and I really love how my photos came out
