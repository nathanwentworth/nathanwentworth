Late post but it's still Monday!! Okay honestly nottttttt an eventful week. That's okay though!!! Air quality was super bad because of wildfires in Canada, very hazy and smoky for a lot of the week, or honestly it was kinda more scary that the worst air quality was on the day that it didn't even look that bad outside?

I went to go see The Invite with coworker Brina, I hadn't really been sold on it from the trailers but it was good!!! Very very funny. Though I honestly was kinda shocked that it was so explicitly about sex and relationships and there was no actual sex in it lmao?? 

Speaking of movies on Saturday I went birding early in the morning, though when I woke up I felt pretty bad. Like, not normal "waking up tired" but especially exhausted. But I thought "well maybe I just need to get outside and then I'll wake up". Quite the opposite!! I biked over to where David was and I instantly felt nauseous and had to sit down for 15 minutes, completely drenched in sweat and my hands going numb. After a bit all of that subsided but I still didn't feel great, and we had kinda missed the birding window anyway, so we both just went home lmao. The fact that I saw three cybertrucks on my bike really should have been an omen. I then proceeded to spend the entire day in bed watching The Wolf of Wall Street which I really did not like, and The One (starring Jet Li) which ruled in a cheesy goofy way. You know an action movie is good when it has a nu-metal soundtrack and has dudes being air-juggled. 

Then Sunday I was watching [Slow & Sacred Films For My Funeral](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mU-Y0T5vM0k) from Youtube User Almost_Orpheus and him talking about Goodbye, Dragon Inn (a movie I've been meaning to watch for a while) got me to watch the original Dragon Inn. Super fun! The first hour is basically flawless, Xiao Shaozi's (Shih Chun) swag is off the charts.
